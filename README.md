# Digital Poker Chips ♠️♥️♣️♦️

A lightweight, open-source, and browser-based digital poker chip tracker. Designed for those spontaneous Texas Hold'em games when you have cards but no physical chips.

## 🚀 Live Demo
You can use the app directly in your browser without downloading anything:
**[Link to App](https://eneskaplans.github.io/burakbabappro/)**

## ✨ Features
- **No Installation Required:** Runs instantly in any mobile or desktop browser (Safari, Chrome, etc.).
- **Mobile Friendly:** Specifically designed to work perfectly on iOS and Android devices.
- **Simplified UI:** Focus on your cards while the app handles the stacks and pots.
- **100% Free & Open-source:** No ads, no paywalls, just poker.
